Rob Dixon 2 upvotes by Mian Muhammad Arslan Yaqub and Peter Martin .

In August 2013 Facebook announced a new Unity SDK that makes it much easier to integrate your Unity game with Facebook. It also provides a simplified install sequence for people who need to download the Unity Web Player plug-in.

At the moment Unity only lists 6 games in their Gallery that are on Facebook, but there are clearly more than that: Unity - Game List.

Unity "sunsetted" their Flash Player support last April, so if they want Unity games to prevail on the Facebook platform they will continue to partner with Facebook to simplify the installation experience for all players.
